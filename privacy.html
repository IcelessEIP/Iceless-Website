Privacy Policy

Iceless is a dating app with video games to help users connect. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Age Restriction

Iceless is intended only for users who are 18 years of age or older. By using the app, you confirm that you are at least 18.

What information do we collect?

  • Username
  • Phone number
  • Gender
  • Pictures
  • Other profile details you provide

How do we use your information?

  • To create your profile and allow you to match with other users
  • To enable messaging and gameplay features
  • To improve the app experience

Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Iceless does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the app.

Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and data, please follow the instructions at iceless.app/report.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at iceless.app/report.
