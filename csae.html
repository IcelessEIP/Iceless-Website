Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Commitment to Protecting Children

We are committed to safeguarding children from all forms of sexual abuse and exploitation. Our app upholds the highest standards of safety and accountability to ensure a secure environment for all users, especially minors.

Policy Overview

Our standards against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) encompass the following key areas:

Zero Tolerance Policy

We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy against any content or behavior that promotes, depicts, or facilitates CSAE.

Content Monitoring and Reporting

Our app allows users to exchange text messages and voice recordings. We take reports of suspicious content or behavior seriously, and such reports are reviewed promptly. Users can report any concerns by contacting us at contact@iceless.app.

Partnerships with Law Enforcement

We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies to report any CSAE-related incidents and ensure swift legal action.

Age Verification and Parental Controls

Iceless is intended for users 18 years of age and older. Measures are in place to help prevent unauthorized access by minors. Additional safeguards may be implemented as our platform evolves.

User Education

We educate users on safe online practices and the importance of reporting inappropriate content.

Data Privacy and Protection

Personal data is protected in accordance with stringent privacy laws, minimizing risks to children and all users.

Employee Training

Our team is trained to identify and respond to CSAE concerns, ensuring swift and effective action.

Regular Policy Review

This policy is reviewed regularly to ensure compliance with evolving legal standards and best practices.

Reporting and Support

If you encounter any CSAE-related content or behavior on our platform:

  • Report it immediately by emailing contact@iceless.app.
  • If necessary, report directly to law enforcement in your region.

Collaboration for a Safer Internet

We encourage users, organizations, and communities to work together in combating CSAE. By staying vigilant and proactive, we can create a safer online environment for everyone.

For further information or inquiries, please contact us at contact@iceless.app.
